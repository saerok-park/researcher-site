Saerok Park

I study self-regulated learning, learning analytics, multimodal trace data, and AI-supported learning environments. My work asks how learners think, monitor, and adapt in complex science and STEM contexts, and how educational technologies can provide timely, equitable support.

About

Research and Education

I am a doctoral student in Learning Sciences and Psychological Studies at the UNC School of Education. My research examines how learners regulate cognition, motivation, and behavior across AI-supported, immersive, game-based, and simulation-based learning environments.

Research Focus

Understanding and supporting self-regulated learning through AI and learning analytics.

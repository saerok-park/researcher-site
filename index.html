About

Research and Education

My work focuses on how learners regulate cognition, motivation, and behavior in complex learning environments. I study trace data, multimodal measures, and large language model-supported tools to better understand when learners need support and how learning technologies can provide timely, equitable feedback.

Research Areas

  • Self-regulated learning and learning analytics
  • Large language models for learner support and feedback
  • Multimodal data in science and STEM learning contexts
  • Game-based, immersive, and simulation-based learning environments
